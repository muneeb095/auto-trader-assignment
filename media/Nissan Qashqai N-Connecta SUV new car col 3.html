Our review of the Nissan Qashqai

4.5
Rating: 4.5 out of 5 stars

"The Qashqai is one the UKs best-selling cars and Nissan wants to keep it that way. With this latest round of updates, the Qashqai gains a new front grille, tweaks to the trim levels and some nifty technology, but will this be enough to fight off strong competition from the likes of the Ford Kuga and Kia Sportage? While rivals offer an array of different fuel types, Nissan sells the Qashqai in two guises. You can have a mild hybrid, or for a unique twist on electrification theres e-Power. This system gives smooth driving with the instant acceleration of an electric car but without the need to plug in. Its pleasant and easy to drive, has some genuinely useful new camera features, and makes an intriguing option for those not ready to buy an electric car."
